About Kyiv Flower Show 2020

In 2020, the best florists from all over Ukraine will gather in Spivoche Pole again to present the most interesting, fragrant, and beautiful flowers.

Kyiv Flower Show is a traditional exhibition that has been held for 32 years in a row. Here you can familiarize yourself with the latest achievements of plant science, see new varieties of flowers, and enjoy charming compositions.

  • The largest flower show in Ukraine
  • A huge number of beautiful flower arrangements
  • The best opportunity to enjoy the aesthetics of flowers
